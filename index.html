About Us

At U FIRST LOANZ, we specialize in helping businesses secure the right financial support through our trusted network of banks and financial institutions. We do not lend directly but act as a bridge—guiding clients toward the best loan options that suit their unique needs.

Our role is to simplify the borrowing journey. From understanding requirements to connecting with the right lender, we ensure a smooth, transparent, and hassle-free experience. Whether it’s working capital, term loans, overdrafts, or project funding, we assist businesses in accessing the right financial solutions quickly and efficiently.

With customer-first service and a strong focus on trust, we aim to be the reliable partner that businesses can count on when it comes to securing finance for growth and success.

What We Do?

We help businesses secure the right loans and working capital through our trusted banking partners. With transparent guidance and customer-focused service, we make the loan process simple, reliable, and hassle-free.

Business Loan

Quick and easy funding to manage working capital, expand operations, or cover day-to-day business needs.
.

Term Loan

Get a lump sum with fixed repayments—ideal for expansion, equipment purchase, or long-term projects.
.

Overdraft (OD) / Unsecured Overdraft

Withdraw funds beyond your account balance anytime. A flexible solution to manage short-term needs.

Cash Credit (CC)

Flexible working capital finance that lets you withdraw funds as per your business requirements.
.

Dropline Overdraft

An overdraft facility with a reducing limit, helping you manage cash flow with disciplined repayment.
